A 3D model based on my original concept, created as the final project for the 3D Modeling course at Masaryk University.

Moora is a sci-fi robot inspired by nature, designed to help restore ecosystems affected by long-term ecological decline. Set in a world where once-healthy landscapes have turned into fragile moorlands, its purpose is to monitor environmental conditions and support the recovery of natural balance.

## Design
Moora combines organic elements with functional elements:
* Moth-like body shape
* High-visibility caution coloring (inspired by nature)
* Soil-contact sensor – measures moisture, pH, and nutrient levels
* Optical system – proximity scanning and infrared imaging (leaf health, water stress)
* Watering module – integrated nozzle and storage tank
* Wing-mounted sensors – solar energy collection and environmental data gathering
* GPS antennas – navigation
